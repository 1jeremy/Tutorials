TradingCalendar class can help us find all the available holidays during a period of time. GetDaysByType returns trading days of the specified TradingDayType that contains trading events associated with the range of dates. Here we choose the type to be PublicHoliday and list all holidays from today to the next two days. 

def OnData(self, data):
    calendar = self.TradingCalendar.GetDaysByType(TradingDayType.PublicHoliday, self.Time, self.Time+timedelta(days=2))
    holidays = [i.Date for i in calendar]

The investment vehicle is SPDR S&P500 ETF. The algorithm will trade the ETF if there are holidays within the next two days and stays in cash during other trading days. 

if not self.Portfolio.Invested and len(holidays)>0:
    self.SetHoldings("SPY", 1)
elif self.Portfolio.Invested and len(holidays)==0:
    self.Liquidate()
