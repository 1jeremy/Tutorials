The momentum effect is one of the most used trading anomalies. It states that what was strongly going up in the near past will probably continue to go up shortly. The momentum effect is strong, but it can suffer significant drawdowns sometimes. There are research papers show that the momentum is sensible to the state of the market. This state of the market can be defined in various ways like the sentiment, prior market returns and so on. Therefore, this algorithm will combine the momentum effect with the market state filter to turn off the momentum trading in down market state times.
